ABOUT ME

I'm Mashu.


I'm a 24 year old furry artist that draws and 3D models! I enjoy both of those as my main hobby, as well as playing some fun non-competitive video games :3. I enjoy listening to Weezer and Asian Kung-Fu Generation as I draw or model. Here's my music playlist.
In case you didn't quite notice, this website was made by me ;3! Web development is just a small skill I picked up as a hobby back in 2018, thanks to my friend that moved to China.

My Story


Originally, I was a furry hater. I found them quite cringe, (still do sometimes) and was quite judgemental. Back in 2018 when I was 17, I bought this cool game called A Hat in Time. I had bought it around the same time I was playing Mario Odyssey, and completely stopped playing Odyssey in favor of this game. Eventually, the time came when they released a new DLC for A Hat in Time, Nyakuza metro. It's a little weeby type Yakuza train metro map. I found it incredibly fun. After that DLC was released, and I had finished it, the A Hat in Time community Discord hosted a mod jam! This was my first experience with making game mods. I really enjoyed the little cat ears and mask that Hat Kid had to wear in that DLC, so I decided to make a mod that changed all of her hats to be the same as that. Here is that mod. So that was a little scary, since I had used Blender once before when I was 13, and I had no clue what I was doing. But the community helped me out! They were so cool and patient with me. You can see the before and after pictures on the steam mod page I linked earlier, one showing green glowing oval eye shapes, then finally the triangle shapes around the cat ears.
After making a bunch of mods, my sister gifted the game OneShot to me. I won't go too far into the details, but I really enjoyed the game, and HAD to make a 3D model of Niko. I added them into A Hat in Time, and it got a few thousand subscribers :^). That made me pretty happy.
I continued to model characters, including my own.
All during that time of modeling and modding, I made a "sona". It wasn't really furry, at least I denied it for so long since he had human skin still, but he was a mix between a cat and a bird, and was mostly furless.
I was a big furry denier in '21. Eventually, I decided that I didn't really want to draw feathers anymore, since that was difficult, so I made him just all cat. And very blue and full of fur. For those who care, this is how I got to what I do now!
